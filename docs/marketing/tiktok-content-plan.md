# Wearit — TikTok / Reels Content Plan (EN, zero-budget)

Goal: first paying users from organic short video. The product output (a
before/after of *you* in clothes) is the asset — every video is a demo.

## Operating principles

1. **Hook in 1.5s or you're dead.** First frame = a question or a transformation,
   not a logo. Put bold on-screen text immediately.
2. **Show the magic, not the app.** Lead with the before/after reveal; the UI is
   secondary. Screen-record the real flow, no narration needed.
3. **Volume > polish.** Post 1–2×/day for 30 days. TikTok rewards consistency and
   tests each video; one in ~15 breaks out. Treat it as at-bats.
4. **Native, not an ad.** Talk like a person who found a tool, not a brand.
5. **One CTA, soft.** "Link in bio" or "it's called Wearit" — don't hard-sell.
6. **Re-use winners.** When one hook pops, remake it 5 ways (new garment, new
   person, new text). Don't invent — duplicate what worked.

## The 10 scripts

Format per script: **Hook (on-screen + spoken) → Beats → CTA.**

### 1. The "haul without buying" hook
- **Hook text:** "POV: you try on the whole Zara new-in without spending $1"
- **Beats:** paste 4–5 Zara product links → rapid before/after of each on you →
  react to the worst one.
- **CTA:** "I use Wearit — link in bio"
- *Why it works:* haul format + zero-risk shopping curiosity.

### 2. The AI verdict drama
- **Hook:** "I let an AI decide if I should buy this dress 👀"
- **Beats:** try on → reveal the Buy / Maybe / **Skip** verdict + score → if it
  says Skip, act betrayed.
- **CTA:** "would you have bought it? it's Wearit"
- *Why:* judgment + suspense = comments ("the AI was right tho").

### 3. Stop returning clothes
- **Hook:** "I returned $400 of clothes last month. never again."
- **Beats:** talk to camera → "now I try everything on first" → show 3 quick
  try-ons, one obvious fail you avoided.
- **CTA:** "Wearit. link in bio."
- *Why:* names a real, expensive pain (returns/shipping).

### 4. Size recommendation
- **Hook:** "the AI told me my real size and it was NOT what I order"
- **Beats:** try on → zoom the recommended size + reason → "I always order M…"
- **CTA:** soft.
- *Why:* sizing anxiety is universal; practical payoff.

### 5. Outfit battle (audience vote)
- **Hook:** "Outfit A or B? AI is breaking the tie"
- **Beats:** two looks on you → ask viewers to comment → show the AI scores.
- **CTA:** "comment A or B 👇"
- *Why:* engagement bait → comments boost reach. Pin your own answer.

### 6. "Try your celebrity crush's outfit"
- **Hook:** "putting [celeb]'s outfit on myself with AI"
- **Beats:** screenshot a celeb fit → try it on you → verdict.
- **CTA:** soft.
- *Why:* borrowed reach from a trending name/look.

### 7. Boyfriend / friend makeover
- **Hook:** "I redressed my boyfriend without him knowing"
- **Beats:** use his photo → try 3 outfits → show him reacting.
- **CTA:** soft.
- *Why:* relationship content travels; two-person = shareable.

### 8. The wedding-guest / event panic
- **Hook:** "wedding in 3 days and nothing to wear — watch"
- **Beats:** paste a few dress links → try on → pick the winner with AI.
- **CTA:** "Wearit saved me. link in bio."
- *Why:* high-intent, time-boxed need = conversions.

### 9. Thrift / Vinted check
- **Hook:** "before you buy that Vinted item, do THIS"
- **Beats:** take the seller photo → try it on yourself → verdict.
- **CTA:** soft.
- *Why:* secondhand shoppers can't try on — direct pain.

### 10. Referral flex (drives the loop)
- **Hook:** "free way to try on clothes (no card)"
- **Beats:** show the 1 free try-on → "comment 'link' and I'll send my invite —
  you get 3 free credits."
- **CTA:** reply with your `/signup?ref=<uid>` link.
- *Why:* turns viewers into referred sign-ups → both get credits → compounding.

## Posting cadence (30-day skeleton)

- **Daily:** 1 post min, 2 ideal (e.g., 11am + 7pm local US).
- **Week 1:** scripts 1–5, one per day, A/B the hooks.
- **Week 2:** remake the 2 best performers 3 ways each + scripts 6–8.
- **Week 3:** double down on the breakout format; introduce 9–10 + referral push.
- **Week 4:** trend-jack (use trending sounds on your winning format).
- Cross-post every video to Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts (zero extra cost).

## Profile setup

- **Handle/bio:** "See it on you before you buy 👗" + link in bio (to `/signup`,
  or a Linktree with `/signup?ref=<your-uid>` so even your own follows refer).
- **Pinned videos:** your 3 clearest before/after reveals.
- Reply to every "what's this app?" comment with the name — comments drive reach.

## What to measure (weekly)

- **Hook rate** (3-sec view %) — fix the first frame if low.
- **Watch-through + saves/shares** — saves predict reach; shares predict the loop.
- **Profile clicks → signups → first try-on → trial start** — the real funnel.
- Don't chase raw views; chase **trial starts per 1k views**. Kill formats that
  get views but no signups.

## Tie-ins already built in the product

- Share buttons export the real before/after (IG/TikTok/WhatsApp) — use them in
  videos so viewers see the share path.
- Referral card on Profile → grab your `/signup?ref=<uid>` link for script #10.
- Outfit battles → script #5. AI verdict + size → scripts #2/#4.
